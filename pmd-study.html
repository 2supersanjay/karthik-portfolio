Propellant Management Device (PMD) Study


Created a Propellant Management Device (PMD) based on a replicated research study.
PMDs enable reliable cryogenic fuel transfer in microgravity by leveraging surface tension.
Cryogenic fuels produce residual gases due to extremely low boiling points, requiring careful propellant acquisition.
PMDs separate and guide liquid to an outlet, preventing gas ingestion.
Completed full CAD in SolidWorks and Volume of Fluid (VOF) CFD simulation in ANSYS FLUENT.

Engineering Process

  • CAD reconstruction in SolidWorks
  • Defeaturing + watertight export
  • Hybrid mesh generation (tet/prism)
  • VOF multiphase modeling in ANSYS Fluent
  • Mesh independence + validation
